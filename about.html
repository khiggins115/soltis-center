Hi!

I'm Katie Higgins! This is my portfolio where I share some my favorite work. When I'm not in school making maps and thinking about how the Earth is actually shaped like a lumpy potato, I'm designing things, learning more about programming, and reading good books.

In case you're interested, I'm currently a senior studying Geographic Information Science Technology (Computation, Design and Analysis Track) with a minor in English Lit. What does all of this mean? It means I'm able to understand technically complex concepts, I relate pretty much any kind of data to its spatial component, and I'm an awesome communicator/writer of things (I've participated in the Texas Grand Slam Poetry festival, for starters).
